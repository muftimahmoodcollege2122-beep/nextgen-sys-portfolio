What I Build

Every paper register, eliminated.

Not a template, not a resold product. Every system is built fresh for your institution.

STD
Student Management
Full profiles, roll numbers, class and section assignment, bulk CSV import, and searchable records across thousands of students.
ATT
Attendance System
Daily attendance in under 2 minutes per class. Auto-WhatsApp to parents when a student is absent. Monthly reports auto-calculated.
FEE
Fee Management
Digital fee ledger, auto-receipts, defaulter lists with reminders. Online payment via EasyPaisa, JazzCash and bank transfer.
RES
Results & Report Cards
Teachers enter marks, system calculates percentage, grade and position. Auto-generates branded PDF report cards parents download directly.
SCH
Timetable & Exam Schedule
Digital class timetables per section, exam schedules with date and venue. One-click WhatsApp blast notifies all parents instantly.
WEB
Public School Website
Branded multi-page site with admissions, news, gallery, faculty and alumni. Replaces printed prospectus and notice boards permanently.
PAR
Parent Portal
Parents check fees, attendance and results anytime. Submit leave requests. Pay fees from phone. Download receipts — no school visit needed.
TCH
Teacher Portal
Teachers mark attendance, post homework, upload bulk results and view their class students from a dedicated panel with their own login.
PRO
Year-End Promotion
One click promotes entire classes. Class 12 students auto-pass out and are added to alumni. Full preview before confirming.

I'm a full-stack developer from D.I. Khan building software that fixes real operational problems for Pakistani educational institutions. Nextgen Sys. started after seeing how much time schools waste on paper registers, handwritten receipts and printed notice slips.

Every system is custom. Your institution's name, logo, colors and workflow are built in from day one. I use a Node.js and SQLite stack fast enough to handle millions of records while cheap enough to run on a single Railway server with no ongoing infrastructure cost for you.

I understand the Pakistani education market — KPK Board grading, local payment methods (JazzCash, EasyPaisa), and the budget realities of schools outside major cities. I'm not selling you enterprise software priced for Lahore Grammar School.
